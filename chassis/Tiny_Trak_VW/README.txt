# Summary

The Volkswagen Bus is one of the coolest models out there in my opinion.
https://youtu.be/hmEx5F9kanw

Also check out the Wheelie bar made for this model by Flyboymac89 https://www.thingiverse.com/thing:3506571https://www.thingiverse.com/thing:3506571

I remixed the original tiny trak wheel base (https://www.thingiverse.com/thing:2896083) and use the wheels from this model (https://www.thingiverse.com/thing:2977620).
Non driven wheels are attached with m3 bolts and nuts. I used extra spacers to align the wheels.

I am still waiting for a mini FrSky RX and a small 5v step up converter, which should arrive soon. These do fit inside the body including a 400mAh 1s lipo.

I am temporary using a bigger dsm2 rx and i scavenged the insides of a cheap powerbank as a 5v converter.

The body and roof can be detached from the base. I used 4mmx2mm round neodymium magnets to attach them. 

I will also make a roof with camera holder.

For the motors i used cheap 9g servos and "modded" these into full rotation.
